Reception Location Options

Hotel

Many hotel options offer options, though smaller venues, to host your
wedding reception.

Local Meeting Houses

We are willing to work with you and local LDS leaders to help book your
wedding reception at a local LDS meeting house.

Reception Food Options

Hotel

For additional cost the hotels that are able to host wedding venus will also
be able to provide meal and refreshments options for your reception.

Though specific information on menu items vary from location to location,
all are able to occomidate any dietary concern.

Local Venders

If you are planning your reception to be located in a local LDS meeting house
local venders are the best option available to you.

These venders array from simple food only options
to fully staffed catering companies that will both set up and take down.

As far as food options, each vender has their own list
of choices and pricing, all information we can provide to you.
